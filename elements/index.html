Pixellens, established in

23 MARCH 2022

Pixellens is the brainchild of two distinguished alumni, Praveen Boss and Bharat Boss. Rooted in their shared vision and passion for filmmaking, Pixellens was founded with the aim of nurturing a vibrant community of aspiring filmmakers within Our University and beyond.

Main Goal of Pixellens is

Cultivating Talent:

Pixellens aims to provide a nurturing environment where aspiring filmmakers can cultivate their talent and develop their skills in various aspects of filmmaking, including directing, cinematography, editing, and sound design.

Creating Opportunities:
